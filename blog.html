Everyone's Favorite Destinations

Kanapolis State Park

This state park is located a short distance outside of Salina Kansas. It has a great area for camping with horses. Most camping sites have full electrical and water hook ups for your convenience. There are great trails to ride although you have to ride a few miles before getting to the good stuff. There are canyons to ride through, water crossings to cross and wooded areas to get out of the sun.

Flying R Ranch

This is a privately owned horse camping facility. They over 800 acres that is backed up on the Mark Twain forest giving over 100 miles of trails to ride. The camp ground facility is the best that we have ever seen. You have complete electrical and water hook ups and there are also 2 horse stalls that come with every camp site.
